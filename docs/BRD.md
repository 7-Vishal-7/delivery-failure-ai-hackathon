# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Title

AI-Assisted Delivery Failure and Reattempt Coordination Workflow

## Business Problem

Online retail delivery failures are often managed manually through spreadsheets, emails, courier portals, and support tickets. This leads to delayed resolutions, inconsistent customer communication, poor visibility into reattempts, and operational inefficiencies.

## Business Objective

Provide a centralized application that enables back-office teams to:

* Capture failed delivery cases
* Classify delivery failure reasons
* Validate delivery information
* Coordinate reattempt deliveries
* Generate AI-assisted summaries and communications
* Maintain auditability and traceability

## Stakeholders

* Back Office Operations Analyst
* Customer Support Executive
* Delivery Coordination Associate
* Warehouse Team
* Courier Partner
* Operations Manager

## Business Requirements

### BR-01

Capture and manage failed delivery cases.

### BR-02

Identify and classify delivery failure reasons.

### BR-03

Validate failed delivery cases using operational checklists.

### BR-04

Generate AI-assisted summaries and recommendations.

### BR-05

Generate customer and courier communications.

### BR-06

Coordinate delivery reattempts.

### BR-07

Maintain complete audit trail and case lifecycle history.

### BR-08

Provide status tracking and operational visibility.
